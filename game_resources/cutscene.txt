Rain-soaked neon streets of Tokyo's Kabukicho district blaze with life. The hero, Kaito, a former Yakuza trying to live clean, his scarred, hardened face silent but eyes flash with defiant spirit. Across the street, the villain, Takeshi, Kaito's ex-brother-in-arms turned ruthless Yakuza boss, smiles devilishly beneath his traditional Irezumi tattoos. Eager for a reckoning, the tension in the air is palpable.
