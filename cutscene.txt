Act 3 sees a hardened Toshi launching an all-out assault on Kazuki's stronghold, a towering skyscraper in the heart of Kabukicho. Toshi fights his way, floor by floor, to Kazuki. A brutal showdown ensues on the skyscraper's rooftop. Driven by the importance of friendship and the need to reclaim peace in Kabukicho, Toshi defeats Kazuki. The game concludes with Toshi rescuing Hana and promising to restore justice and order to the district as the new Yakuza boss.
